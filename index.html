About

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Toronto’s Faculty of Information (iSchool). My research interrogates digital media practices, remix culture, and Islamic devotional communities with a focus on South Asia and its diasporas. My dissertation, “Remixing Devotion: Digital Media Practices in Pakistan,” explores how practitioners navigate piracy, AI, and platform logics to circulate sacred audio-visuals.
